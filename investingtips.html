Investing for the long run

When it comes to investing for the future it is important to know the company your investing in. What i mean by this is, the company has to have long term value. Is the company consistently improving their products? For example, Apple is a prime example of a company that constantly improves. Every IPhone that they came out with has drastically improved, whether it was there software or the physcial features of the phone, appple is constantly improving their products. Fundamentally, Apple is a company that would be great to invest in for long term growth.

Dividend Stocks

Another way companies get more shareholders in their stock is offering dividend when they purchase shares. A dividened is a distrubution of the companies earnings to their shareholders. Dividends are distrubuted quarterly(every three months) and they are paid out directly in cash or the purchase of more shares with the company.

What type of stocks have the best paying dividends?

The companies that are larger in the business world and can easily predict positive revenue quarterly are the best paying dividend companies. The sectors that have the best paying companies in them are:
  1. Basic Materials
  2. Oil and Gas
  3. Banks and Finacials
  4. Healthcare and pharmaceuticals
  5. Utilites
The two best paying dividend stock based on payouts due to great revenue quarterly is Apple and Microsoft. Apple has a paying dividend of .24$ per share. Appple has an annual dividend yield of .56%. Microsoft pays .75$ per share, and has an annual Dividend yield of .71%.
